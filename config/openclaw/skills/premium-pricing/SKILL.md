## Pricing Strategy Framework

**Goal:** position at premium tier — not cheapest, not "affordable" — but justifiably higher with clear value logic

## Pricing Model Selection

| Model | Best For | HU Market Notes |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| Flat project fee | Clear scope, defined deliverables | Preferred by HU SME clients (predictability) |
| Retainer (monthly) | Ongoing services, agency relationships | Growing acceptance in digital/marketing |
| Value-based | High-ROI consulting, transformation projects | Explain ROI before price — HU buyers skeptical |
| Tiered (3 options) | SaaS, productized services | Anchoring effect strong in HU market |
| Per-unit / usage | SaaS, API, data products | Scale with client growth |

## Tier Structure (3-tier default)

| Tier | Name | Position | Price Ratio |
|------|------|---------|------------|
| Tier 1 | Alap / Starter | Loss leader or entry | 1× |
| Tier 2 | Standard / Pro | Sweet spot (target) | 2.5-3× |
| Tier 3 | Prémium / Enterprise | Anchor + upsell | 5-8× |

**Anchoring rule:** Tier 3 price makes Tier 2 look reasonable. Tier 1 provides access point, not profit.

## ROI Framing Template
```
Befektetés: X Ft / hó
Ha [metric] X%-kal javul, az Y Ft / hó többletbevételt jelent.
Megtérülési idő: Z hónap.
```

## Hungarian Market Pricing Signals
- Price in HUF for HU-domiciled clients (trust signal)
- EUR pricing acceptable for international-facing SaaS
- Avoid prices ending in ,990 or ,999 — reads as "discount store" in premium context
- Round numbers (500 000 Ft, 1 200 000 Ft) signal confidence
- Show price AFTER demonstrating value (never lead with price)

## Discount Policy
- Never discount publicly listed prices (erodes anchor value)
- Volume/commitment discount: acceptable if tied to longer contract
- Early adopter pricing: time-limited with sunset date
- Document all non-standard pricing to prevent scope creep reference

## Output Expectations
- Recommended pricing model with rationale
- 3-tier table with prices, inclusions, and positioning names
- ROI framing paragraph (ready to paste into proposal)
- Discount policy recommendation

## Quality Criteria
- Tier 2 price defensible with value argument (not just "market rate")
- ROI calculation based on client-provided or researched metrics (not assumed)
- Pricing consistent with positioning — no premium brand at commodity price
