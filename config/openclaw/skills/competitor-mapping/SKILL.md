## Competitor Research Scope

**Tiers to analyze:**
- **Tier 1:** Direct competitors (same audience, same solution)
- **Tier 2:** Indirect competitors (same audience, different solution)
- **Tier 3:** Alternative solutions (how audience solves problem without a competitor)

**Research sources:**
- Company website (positioning, pricing if public, ICP signals)
- G2, Capterra, Trustpilot reviews (real user language, pain points, gaps)
- LinkedIn (team size, growth signals, recent hires → strategic direction)
- App stores, Chrome extensions (usage signals)
- Hungarian: itthon.hu, opten.hu (company financials), LinkedIn HU

## Gap Analysis Output
- **Blue ocean opportunities:** needs unaddressed by any competitor
- **Table stakes:** features clients expect from every player (must-have)
- **Differentiators:** features where meaningful gap exists to exploit
- **Vulnerabilities:** top complaints in competitor reviews = our opportunity

## Output Expectations
- Completed analysis matrix (all tiers, all dimensions)
