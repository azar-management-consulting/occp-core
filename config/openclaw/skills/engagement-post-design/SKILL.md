## Engagement Post Types

Select type based on goal:
- **Opinion/debate post:** drives comments (agree/disagree dynamic)
- **List/tips post:** drives saves
- **Story post:** drives shares (emotionally resonant narrative)
- **Poll/question post:** drives direct engagement (comments, reactions)
- **Contrast post:** "Before vs. After", "Myth vs. Reality" — drives saves + shares

## Post Construction Rules

**Opening line (first 2 lines — before "Tovább"):**
- Must create information gap or emotional hook
- Question format outperforms statement for cold audiences
- Controversial or counterintuitive claim for warm audiences

**Body structure:**
- Short paragraphs: max 3 sentences per paragraph
- Line breaks every 2-3 lines (wall of text = scroll past)
- White space is a readability tool on mobile
- Bold key phrases with asterisks (*szöveg*) for emphasis

**Closing mechanics:**
Choose one engagement trigger:
- Comment invitation: "Mit gondolsz? Írd le kommentbe."
- Save trigger: "Mentsd el, mert visszajössz hozzá."
- Share trigger: "Küld el valakinek, akinek szüksége van erre."
- Follow trigger: "Kövess, ha [specific benefit of following]."

## Platform-Specific Notes
- **Facebook:** longer form works (300-500 words), story posts drive organic reach
- **Instagram:** 150-220 characters before fold, emoji used sparingly for B2B
- **LinkedIn HU:** formal `Ön` optional, professional credibility signals required
- **TikTok caption:** 150 characters max effective, rest is hashtags

## Timing and Frequency
- Engagement posts: max 2× per week (overuse dilutes response rate)
- Best times for HU audience: Tue-Thu 11:00-13:00 and 19:00-21:00

## Output Expectations
- Post copy for chosen platform (ready to publish)
- Engagement trigger type identified
- 2 headline/opening line variations
- Hashtag set if required (platform-appropriate count)

## Quality Criteria
- Post adds standalone value (not purely promotional)
- Engagement trigger is explicit and specific
- Tone consistent with brand voice (formal/informal confirmed in brief)
